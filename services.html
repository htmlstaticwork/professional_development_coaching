Service & Pricing FAQ

Clear answers about how our billing and sessions operate.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier later?
Yes, our plans are entirely flexible month-to-month. If you find you need more intensive support ahead of a major interview, you can toggle up to Accelerate. If you need a break, you can pause or downgrade at any time with a 14-day notice.
Are the roleplay sessions recorded?
Absolutely. Every strategic 1:1 and interview roleplay is recorded and provided to you alongside timestamped critical feedback so you can continuously re-watch and adapt your pacing.
Do you offer payment plans for Corporate Training?
For corporate seminars and large-scale Mastermind cohort bookings, we operate on a 50/50 retainer structure. 50% upfront to hold the calendar dates, and the remaining 50% distributed upon successful completion of the delivery. Let's discuss specific terms during our consultation.
