Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coaching methodology.

Is 1:1 Coaching right for me if I'm not in a leadership role yet?
Absolutely. Our programs are designed to help you transition into leadership seamlessly. The earlier you adopt executive strategies, the faster your trajectory will be. We focus on building the foundational confidence and presence required to step up.
How much time do I need to commit weekly?
Expect to commit around 1-2 hours per week for live sessions and review, plus an additional hour for independent application of the frameworks in your daily work environment. Our approach maps directly to your existing workload.
Does my company usually cover the cost?
Yes, over 65% of our clients receive full or partial reimbursement through their company's Professional Development or L&D budgets. We provide comprehensive sponsorship request templates upon inquiry.
